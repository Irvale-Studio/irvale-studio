## Lead with the answer to the title's question

<Speakable>
The 2–3 sentence direct answer goes here. Lead with a number, a band, or a yes/no. AI engines lift these sentences as the canonical answer when this query is searched in ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity.
</Speakable>

Below the answer paragraph, expand on the *why* and *how*. Use shorter paragraphs (40–80 words each) and break long sections with H3 sub-headings. Remember to keep H1 → H2 → H3 strictly hierarchical — no skipping levels.
