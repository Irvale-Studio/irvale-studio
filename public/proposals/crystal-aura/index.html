05 · What you can expect

Calibrated expectations, monthly reporting, no fairy tales.

Digital growth compounds. The first months are foundation work; the gains start landing once the engine is running.

Months 1–2

Foundation in place. Profile views and search impressions begin rising as new content and listings get indexed. Booking volume largely flat — this is groundwork, and we'll be transparent about that.

Months 3–4

Compounding effects begin. Naver Place views, Xiaohongshu impressions, and GBP views climb. First incremental bookings attributable to new channels start to land. Review velocity picks up.

Months 5–6

Measurable lift in direct bookings — bypassing third-party commissions and rebuilding margin. Established presence in Chinese and Korean discovery channels. Reduced reliance on GoWabi and Klook for new-customer acquisition.

Months 6+

Compounding continues. Most service businesses see a 20–50% revenue lift from a fully integrated digital funnel within twelve months. We treat that as a benchmark, not a guarantee — your actual result depends on operational consistency, market conditions, and your own competitive context.
