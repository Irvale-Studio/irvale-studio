01 · The brief

Black Cap Production — Website Upgrade.

What works today

  • Strong service breadth: full production, fixer, crew, equipment, locations, casting, post.
  • Recognisable client work (Mastercard, TAT) sits inside the page.
  • Clean minimalist tone, mobile-responsive, direct WhatsApp contact.
  • Positioning is clear: Thailand-based, SEA-capable, B2B production partner.

Where the gap sits

  • Two-page footprint. No project pages, no service pages, no case study depth.
  • Videos and stills are flat in the page. Video-led work needs a video-led site.
  • No schema markup, no sitemap structure. Effectively invisible to AI search.
  • Performance and security headers untuned. Brand reads premium; metrics don't.
  • One conversion path (WhatsApp). Inbound leaks for users who research before contacting.
